## Cybersecurity Compliance

Adherence to laws, regulations, and standards designed to protect digital assets, data privacy, and information systems. Involves implementing risk-based controls to ensure data confidentiality, integrity, and availability — reducing breach risks and avoiding legal penalties.

---

## Regulations

| Acronym | Full Name | Scope |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| PCI DSS | Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard | Payment card data protection |
| HIPAA | Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 | Healthcare data privacy |
| GDPR | General Data Protection Regulation | EU citizen data protection |
| CCPA | California Consumer Privacy Act | California resident privacy rights |
| SOX | Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 | Financial reporting integrity |

---

## Key Takeaways

| Item                 | Detail                                                |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Compliance goal      | Protect digital assets and avoid legal penalties      |
| Risk-based approach  | Controls proportional to threat level                 |
| CIA triad foundation | Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability              |
| Geographic scope     | Regulations vary by region (EU, US federal, US state) |
