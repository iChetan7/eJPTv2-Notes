## What is Social Engineering?

A **cybersecurity discipline** focused on manipulating people into breaking normal security procedures — using psychological tactics to gain unauthorized access to systems, data, or physical spaces. This course covers fundamentals, real-world case studies, and hands-on labs including phishing and watering-hole attacks.

| Objective | Description |
